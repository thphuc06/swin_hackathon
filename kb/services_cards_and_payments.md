# Banking Services - Cards And Payments

## Scope
Educational guidance for cards, payment behavior, and expense control services.

## Service Categories

### 1) Debit Card
- Purpose: spend from available account balance.
- Advisory use:
- Suggest for budget discipline and low debt risk.

### 2) Credit Card
- Purpose: short credit cycle, rewards, and convenience.
- Advisory use:
- Suggest only with control conditions:
- Statement full payment habit.
- Spending cap by category.
- Alerting and due-date reminders enabled.

### 3) Installment Conversion
- Purpose: spread large purchase cost over periods.
- Advisory use:
- Suggest with caution when purchase is essential and cash buffer remains healthy.

### 4) Auto Debit / Bill Payment Setup
- Purpose: reduce missed-payment risk for fixed obligations.
- Advisory use:
- Suggest when user has recurring bills and late-fee incidents.

## Behavioral Controls Advisor Can Recommend
- Weekly spend review for top 3 categories.
- Per-category monthly budget cap.
- Real-time notifications for unusual transactions.
- Merchant blocklist for leakage categories.

## Data Needed Before Suggesting Category
- Spend concentration by category and merchant.
- Over-spend propensity signal.
- Payment punctuality indicators.
- Cashflow volatility.

## Compliance Notes
- No promotion language.
- No specific card recommendation without current fee/reward data.
- Explain tradeoff: convenience vs over-spend risk.
