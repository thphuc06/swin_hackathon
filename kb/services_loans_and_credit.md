# Banking Services - Loans And Credit

## Scope
Educational guidance for loan-related service categories.
No direct recommendation to borrow. No execution instruction.

## Service Categories

### 1) Home Loan (Vay mua nha)
- Purpose: finance home purchase with long tenor.
- Typical checks:
- Debt-to-income ratio.
- Down payment readiness.
- Income stability and repayment capacity.
- Advisory use:
- Suggest as category when user asks housing affordability path.

### 2) Personal Installment Loan
- Purpose: medium-term borrowing for planned expense.
- Typical checks:
- Monthly repayment burden under stress scenarios.
- Emergency buffer after repayment.
- Advisory use:
- Suggest when user has clear use-case and stable cashflow.

### 3) Overdraft / Credit Line
- Purpose: short-term liquidity bridge.
- Typical checks:
- Frequency of negative balance.
- Expected time to recover cashflow.
- Advisory use:
- Use as cautionary category for temporary cash mismatch, not long-term solution.

### 4) Debt Consolidation
- Purpose: simplify multiple debts and optimize repayment schedule.
- Typical checks:
- Total outstanding debt and blended cost.
- Ability to commit to new repayment plan.
- Advisory use:
- Suggest when user has fragmented obligations and late-fee risk.

## Risk Signals Advisor Should Explain
- High repayment ratio vs monthly net income.
- Negative net cashflow trend over multiple periods.
- Low emergency runway with rising fixed expenses.

## Data Needed Before Suggesting Loan Category
- Monthly disposable income.
- Existing debt obligations.
- Emergency runway.
- Goal urgency and fallback options.

## Compliance Notes
- Do not promise approval.
- Do not provide exact loan pricing without verified source.
- Always include repayment-risk explanation and disclaimer.
