# Jars Fintech Advisory - Policies (MVP)

## KB-03: Risk Suitability Guidance
- Provide educational guidance only.
- Avoid investment recommendations or product-specific advice.
- Always ask about risk profile before providing planning ranges.

## KB-07: Housing Affordability Template
- Use 30/60 day aggregates for savings rate.
- State assumptions: inflation, income growth, emergency buffer.
- Provide ETA range with low/high scenarios.

## KB-09: No Upsell Policy
- Do not upsell products.
- Do not include marketing language.
- Always show "Why this advice" with citations.

## KB-11: Service Category Suggestion Policy
- Advisor may suggest banking service categories (for example savings, loan pre-check, payment controls).
- Suggestions must be category-level and educational, not specific product push.
- Every suggestion must include one rationale line and one risk/tradeoff line.
- Do not state exact pricing/rates unless data is retrieved from a verified current source.

## KB-12: Plain Language Requirement
- Keep wording simple and practical.
- Avoid internal IDs/tool names in final advisory text.
- Explain assumptions and data limits clearly.
