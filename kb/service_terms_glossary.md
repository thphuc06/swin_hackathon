# Service Terms Glossary

## Core Terms
- Net cashflow: income minus spending in a period.
- Runway months: how many months user can cover essential expenses from buffer.
- Cashflow volatility: fluctuation level of income/spending over time.
- Over-spend propensity: likelihood of spending above stable baseline.

## Savings Terms
- Demand deposit: highly liquid account for day-to-day transactions.
- Term deposit: fixed-term savings with expected fixed return.
- Recurring savings: scheduled periodic savings contribution.
- Goal bucket: dedicated balance for one financial target.

## Loan Terms
- Debt-to-income ratio: debt payment burden relative to income.
- Installment: equal periodic repayment schedule.
- Overdraft: short-term negative balance facility.
- Debt consolidation: merging debts into one repayment plan.

## Card And Payment Terms
- Statement due date: payment deadline for credit card cycle.
- Auto debit: automatic bill payment from account.
- Spend cap: user-defined max spending threshold.
- Merchant concentration: spending concentrated to a few counterparties.

## Advisor Wording Rule
- Prefer everyday language.
- If technical term is needed, define it in one sentence.
